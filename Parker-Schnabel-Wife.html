Who Is Parker Schnabel’s Wife? Exploring His Love Life and Relationships

Parker Schnabel is a well-known star from the TV show Gold Rush. He’s famous for mining gold in the Yukon and leading his team with skill. Fans love his adventures, but many are curious about his personal life. Does Parker Schnabel have a wife? As of 2025, Parker is not married and has no wife. He’s single, focusing on his mining career, but his past relationships have sparked plenty of interest.

Early Life and Career

Parker was born on July 22, 1994, in Haines, Alaska. He grew up in a family of gold miners, learning from his grandfather, John Schnabel, who owned Big Nugget Mine. Parker started mining at a young age and took over the family business at 16. His hard work paid off, and he now runs his own company, Little Flake Mining. With a net worth of about $8 million, he’s one of the youngest successful miners on Gold Rush.

Parker’s Most Famous Relationship

Many fans wonder about Parker’s love life because he keeps it private. His most famous relationship was with Ashley Youle, an Australian veterinary nurse. They met in Australia in 2016 during a break from filming Gold Rush. Ashley joined Parker in Alaska, working with him on the show for two seasons. Fans loved their chemistry, and some thought she might become Parker’s wife. But in 2018, they broke up. Parker said he didn’t make the relationship a priority, focusing too much on work.

Rumors and Speculations

After Ashley, rumors spread about Parker dating other women. One was Tyler Mahoney, a fellow gold miner from Australia. They worked together on Gold Rush: Parker’s Trail in 2020, and their close bond made fans think they were a couple. Tyler cleared up the rumors, saying they were just friends. She’s now dating someone else. Another rumor linked Parker to Monica Beets, but that was false too, as Monica is married to Taylor Mayes.

Other Rumored Relationships

Parker has also been linked to Sheena Cowell, an assistant producer on Gold Rush, in 2019. However, neither confirmed the relationship, and it faded from the spotlight. Right now, Parker seems happy being single. He told People magazine that describing his job—living in the wilderness and mining gold—makes dating tough. It’s hard to explain his life without it sounding like a wild adventure movie!

Future Plans for Love and Family

Despite his focus on work, Parker hopes to have a family one day. He’s only 30, so there’s plenty of time. He said he might wait until Gold Rush ends to start a family, as his career takes up most of his time. For now, he’s busy chasing gold, aiming for a big 10,000-ounce season worth $25 million in Gold Rush Season 15.

Fans’ Curiosity and Parker’s Focus

Parker’s fans admire his dedication to mining, but they also root for his happiness. His Instagram mostly shows his work and crew, with no hints of a girlfriend. While some fans speculated about a teenage boy in a 2024 photo being his son, it was just a friend’s child. Parker’s life is all about gold, but his story keeps fans hooked, wondering if love will strike again.
